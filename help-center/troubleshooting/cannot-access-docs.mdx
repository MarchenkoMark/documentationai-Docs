---
title: Cannot Access Docs
description: Resolve issues with accessing documentation.
---

## Symptoms

- Documentation page is blank or unavailable
- Access denied error displayed

## Causes

- Not logged in
- Permissions missing

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login">
    Make sure you are signed in to your Mark Marchenko account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request access">
    Contact your admin, or email support if you need documentation access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
